# Backlink Audit Framework

## Audit dimensions

1. Source quality (authority, trust, editorial standards)
2. Topical relevance to target pages and industry
3. Anchor text distribution and over-optimization risk
4. Dofollow/nofollow ratio and placement quality
5. Historical acquisition velocity and anomaly detection

## Risk flags

- High concentration of exact-match anchors
- Large volume of irrelevant or low-quality domains
- Sitewide footer/sidebar links from unrelated properties
- Sudden unnatural growth spikes

## Action buckets

- Keep: high-quality, relevant, stable links
- Monitor: neutral links with uncertain quality
- Clean up/disavow candidate: harmful or manipulative links
