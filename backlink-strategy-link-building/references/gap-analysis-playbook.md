# Competitor Gap and Opportunity Mapping

## Gap analysis method

- Compare referring domains at domain and page level.
- Group opportunities by source class:
  - Editorial publications
  - Niche communities and associations
  - Tool/resource roundups
  - Partner ecosystems
  - Data/report citations

## Prioritization model

Score each opportunity by:

- Relevance fit
- Authority potential
- Acquisition likelihood
- Time-to-win
- Expected business impact

## Output format

For each opportunity:

- target_source
- target_page
- source_type
- relevance_reason
- estimated_difficulty
- recommended_outreach_angle
