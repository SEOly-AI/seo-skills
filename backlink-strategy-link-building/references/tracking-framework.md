# Tracking and Optimization Framework

## KPI layers

### Authority metrics

- Referring domains growth (quality-weighted)
- Authority trend (DR/DA or equivalent)
- Link retention and decay rate

### Search performance

- Ranking lift on target clusters
- Organic clicks/impressions on linked pages
- CTR trend impact where applicable

### Business outcomes

- Assisted conversions from linked landing pages
- Pipeline impact from authority-focused pages

## Iteration loop

1. Measure link quality and outcomes.
2. Remove low-value sources from future targeting.
3. Double down on high-converting source classes.
4. Update outreach messages and asset formats.
