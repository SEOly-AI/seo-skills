# Backlink Strategy Output Rubric

Use this rubric before final delivery. Mark each as **Pass / Partial / Fail**.

## A. Context and Inputs (20%)

- [ ] Context files were reviewed and availability reported.
- [ ] Baseline profile, industry attributes, competitors, and budget are confirmed.
- [ ] Missing-data questions are concise and relevant.

## B. Audit Quality (20%)

- [ ] Link profile audit covers quality, relevance, anchors, and risk.
- [ ] Toxic/risky patterns are explicitly flagged.
- [ ] Keep/monitor/cleanup grouping is actionable.

## C. Gap Analysis and Strategy (25%)

- [ ] Competitor gap map is source-level and prioritized.
- [ ] Strategy includes sustainable acquisition tracks.
- [ ] Relevance and quality thresholds are clearly defined.

## D. Execution Plan (20%)

- [ ] Outreach workflow is concrete (targets, messaging, cadence, ownership).
- [ ] Landing-page mapping avoids over-optimization.
- [ ] Black-hat methods are explicitly excluded.

## E. Tracking and Improvement (15%)

- [ ] KPI framework includes authority, rankings, and business outcomes.
- [ ] Iteration loop is clear and feasible.
- [ ] Risk monitoring and link decay handling are included.

## Hard Fail Conditions

- Recommending manipulative link schemes.
- No relevance filter for acquisition sources.
- Non-actionable strategy without execution detail.
