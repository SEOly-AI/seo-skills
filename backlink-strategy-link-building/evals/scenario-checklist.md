# Scenario Checklist

## S1 - Strong Content but Stalled Rankings

- [ ] Authority gap is diagnosed as the primary constraint.
- [ ] High-impact source categories are prioritized.
- [ ] Link-to-page mapping targets ranking bottlenecks.

## S2 - New Site Cold Start

- [ ] Initial authority build plan is staged by quarter.
- [ ] Early link sources are relevance-first and feasible.
- [ ] Risk controls are in place to avoid low-quality growth.

## S3 - Legacy Site Backlink Optimization

- [ ] Toxic/risky links are identified and treatment plan is provided.
- [ ] Anchor distribution is rebalanced.
- [ ] Recovery plan includes both cleanup and new high-quality acquisition.

## S4 - Content Asset Promotion

- [ ] Asset types (blog/report/tool) have tailored outreach angles.
- [ ] Source targeting aligns with asset intent and audience.
- [ ] Performance tracking ties links to visibility and conversion outcomes.

## S5 - Vertical Industry Authority Building

- [ ] Niche high-trust sources are identified.
- [ ] Industry-specific trust constraints are respected.
- [ ] Long-term authority compounding plan is documented.
