# Scenario Checklist

## S1 - Sudden Ranking Collapse Without Obvious Changes

- [ ] Timeline and segment diagnostics are complete.
- [ ] Candidate external and algorithmic factors are assessed.
- [ ] Immediate containment actions are proposed.

## S2 - Core Update / Panda / Penguin-like Impact

- [ ] Quality/link-related weak points are identified.
- [ ] Recovery roadmap addresses update-specific patterns.
- [ ] Validation windows are realistic.

## S3 - Legacy Black-Hat Cleanup

- [ ] Manipulative tactics are inventoried and prioritized.
- [ ] Cleanup and replacement strategy is defined.
- [ ] Risk of residual penalties is monitored.

## S4 - Migration/Configuration Mistake Recovery

- [ ] Robots/canonical/redirect/index issues are isolated.
- [ ] Fixes are sequenced with rollback safety.
- [ ] Recovery tracking is tied to pre-incident baselines.
