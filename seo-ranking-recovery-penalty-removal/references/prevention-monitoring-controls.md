# Prevention and Monitoring Controls

## Preventive controls

- Pre-release SEO QA for templates and routing
- Change logs linked to performance monitoring
- Policy guardrails against manipulative SEO tactics

## Monitoring setup

- Alert thresholds for traffic/ranking/index anomalies
- Segment-level dashboards for critical pages/queries
- Monthly quality and risk reviews

## No-regression governance

- Post-release verification checklist
- Rollback criteria for severe SEO regressions
- Ownership matrix for incident response
