# Architecture and URL Governance

## Architecture principles

- Keep shallow click depth for critical pages.
- Build clear topical and commercial pathways.
- Avoid disconnected clusters and dead-end routes.

## URL governance

- Normalize trailing slash, case, and parameter handling.
- Define one canonical URL per content intent.
- Minimize dynamic URL exposure to crawlers.

## Redirect standards

- Remove chains and loops.
- Use 301 for permanent migrations.
- Preserve intent and equity to closest matching target.
