# Scenario Coverage Checklist

## S1 - Broad AI Traffic Capture (SaaS/Ecommerce/Blog/Local)

- [ ] Platform priorities are clear (Google/Bing/other).
- [ ] Cluster-level GEO opportunities are prioritized.
- [ ] Site-type-specific constraints are addressed.

## S2 - Good SERP Rankings, Low AI Surface Presence

- [ ] Existing high-ranking URLs audited for AI answer-readiness.
- [ ] Missing citation signals diagnosed.
- [ ] Remediation plan includes content + schema + internal linking.

## S3 - GEO-first New Content Planning

- [ ] New content templates include answer-first structure.
- [ ] Intent ownership is assigned before publishing.
- [ ] Schema requirements are embedded in content briefs.

## S4 - Existing Blog/Tutorial/FAQ Refresh

- [ ] Legacy content clusters selected by impact.
- [ ] AI-writing-pattern cleanup and evidence upgrades applied.
- [ ] Before/after KPI tracking method is defined.
