# GEO + SEO Tracking Framework

## KPI layers

### Layer 1: AI visibility

- AI overview/answer inclusion rate
- Citation frequency by page cluster
- AI-surface impressions and click share (if available)

### Layer 2: Traditional SEO

- Query/page impressions and clicks
- Ranking trends by intent cluster
- CTR changes after GEO updates

### Layer 3: Business impact

- Assisted conversions from optimized clusters
- Lead quality or revenue contribution by topic
- Time-to-impact by content type

## Iteration cycle

1. Observe performance changes.
2. Diagnose weak clusters (intent mismatch, thin evidence, schema gaps).
3. Apply fixes (content, schema, links, consolidation).
4. Re-measure in the next reporting window.
