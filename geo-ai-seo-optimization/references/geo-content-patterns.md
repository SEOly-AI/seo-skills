# GEO Content Optimization Patterns

## Retrieval and generation readiness

For each target page:

1. Add a concise answer-first summary near the top.
2. Use strong heading hierarchy with intent-specific sections.
3. Include explicit entities (brand, product, audience, use case, constraints).
4. Add evidence: stats, references, examples, or methodology.
5. Clarify ownership: one primary page per intent cluster.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- Generic verbose filler with low factual density.
- Repetitive template language across multiple pages.
- Unverifiable claims without evidence or scope.
- Multiple pages targeting the same primary AI-intent query.

## Compatibility with classic SEO

- Keep title/H1 intent-consistent.
- Preserve internal linking depth and crawl paths.
- Maintain canonical strategy and avoid duplicate intent pages.
- Keep metadata quality while structuring for AI extraction.
