# Opportunity Prioritization Model

## Opportunity classes

- High impression + low CTR pages
- Keywords ranking in positions 6-20 with strong intent fit
- High-intent pages with weak conversion rate
- Under-linked but high-value pages
- Content clusters with widening competitor gap

## Prioritization score

Score opportunities by:

- Impact potential
- Implementation effort
- Time-to-impact
- Confidence of diagnosis

## Output format

For each opportunity, include:

- target_segment
- hypothesis
- recommended_action
- expected_metric_impact
- owner
- deadline
