# Distribution and Internal Linking Framework

## Distribution model

- Launch via owned channels (site, newsletter, community, social).
- Refresh and repurpose strong performers across formats.
- Build periodic refresh queue for decaying content.

## Internal linking model

- Link from support topics to pillar pages for authority consolidation.
- Link from informational pages to conversion pages by intent stage.
- Keep anchor text specific and context-aligned.

## Iteration signals

Track:

- ranking progression by cluster
- impressions/clicks/CTR by page type
- conversion contribution by content cohort
