# Content Planning and Briefing

## Brief components

Each brief should include:

- target intent and audience
- primary/secondary query targets
- unique angle and differentiation
- required evidence/examples
- CTA objective and next-step action

## Structural standards

- Use clear H1/H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Include answer-first intro for intent clarity.
- Keep section flow from core answer to depth and proof.
- Predefine internal links to relevant cluster pages.
