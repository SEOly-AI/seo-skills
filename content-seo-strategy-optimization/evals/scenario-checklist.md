# Scenario Checklist

## S1 - Content Site Standardization

- [ ] Topic selection and writing standards are documented.
- [ ] Editorial workflow is repeatable.
- [ ] Internal linking supports topical authority.

## S2 - SaaS/Ecommerce Content Buildout

- [ ] Blog/help/tutorial roadmap aligns with product funnel.
- [ ] High-intent support topics are prioritized.
- [ ] Conversion-oriented CTA pathways are mapped.

## S3 - Existing Low-Performance Content Refresh

- [ ] Low-traffic pages are diagnosed and grouped.
- [ ] Refresh actions are specific (rewrite/restructure/relink/retarget).
- [ ] Pre/post KPI measurement is defined.

## S4 - Topic Cluster Expansion

- [ ] Core pillar and supporting topics are complete.
- [ ] Overlap/cannibalization risks are controlled.
- [ ] Authority growth signals are measurable over time.
