# Content Benchmark Playbook

## Content comparison axes

- Topic coverage breadth and depth
- Intent fit and section architecture quality
- Publishing cadence and freshness behavior
- E-E-A-T evidence and trust signals

## Quality diagnostic prompts

For each competitor page, check:

- Is the answer clear and immediate?
- Is depth sufficient for the query intent?
- Is evidence credible and specific?
- Is the page structurally easy to scan and navigate?

## Output expectations

- list of content advantages to emulate
- list of weaknesses to exploit
- differentiated angle recommendations
