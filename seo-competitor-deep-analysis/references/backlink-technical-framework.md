# Backlink and Technical Benchmark Framework

## Backlink analysis

- Compare referring domain quality and topical relevance.
- Detect acquisition channels and recurring source patterns.
- Map realistic opportunities for replication or differentiation.

## Technical benchmark

- Compare crawl/index architecture and URL governance.
- Assess CWV/mobile readiness at template level.
- Identify technical advantages and vulnerabilities.

## Conversion-path comparison

- Evaluate ranking-page UX and CTA progression.
- Detect friction points that reduce conversion yield.
- Tie SEO visibility to business outcome potential.
