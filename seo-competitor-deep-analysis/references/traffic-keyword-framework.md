# Traffic and Keyword Benchmark Framework

## Traffic benchmarking

- Compare organic visibility by market/device/page type.
- Identify trend divergence and momentum shifts.
- Flag segment-level overperformance by competitor.

## Keyword benchmarking

- Group keywords by intent and funnel stage.
- Compare ranking distribution and share of voice.
- Surface overlap, gap, and whitespace clusters.

## Priority logic

Score opportunities by:

- business relevance
- ranking feasibility
- expected traffic upside
- conversion proximity
