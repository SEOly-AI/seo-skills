# Competitor Analysis Output Rubric

Use this rubric before final delivery. Mark each as **Pass / Partial / Fail**.

## A. Scope and Input Quality (20%)

- [ ] Competitor scope is explicit and justified.
- [ ] Tool/data limitations are transparent.
- [ ] Priority business goals are reflected in analysis focus.

## B. Benchmark Depth (25%)

- [ ] Traffic and keyword benchmarking are segmented and actionable.
- [ ] Content benchmark captures quality and strategic differences.
- [ ] Backlink and technical benchmarks include meaningful evidence.

## C. Diagnostic Clarity (20%)

- [ ] Competitor strengths and weaknesses are clearly separated.
- [ ] Findings are supported by data or observable evidence.
- [ ] Confidence levels are assigned where uncertainty exists.

## D. Strategy Quality (25%)

- [ ] Outperform strategy balances benchmark and differentiation.
- [ ] Prioritization includes impact, effort, and time-to-win.
- [ ] Roadmap is executable with ownership and sequencing.

## E. KPI and Iteration Model (10%)

- [ ] KPI targets are measurable and tied to actions.
- [ ] Review cadence is defined.

## Hard Fail Conditions

- Generic competitor summary without tactical implications.
- No prioritization logic.
- No differentiation path beyond imitation.
