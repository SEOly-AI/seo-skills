# Scenario Checklist

## S1 - New Site Cold Start

- [ ] Top competitor strategy is reverse engineered.
- [ ] Fast-start SEO roadmap is defined.
- [ ] Resource-feasible first milestones are included.

## S2 - Ranking Bottleneck Behind Competitors

- [ ] Core gap drivers are identified by dimension.
- [ ] Priority catch-up and overtake initiatives are clear.
- [ ] Timeline and KPI checkpoints are realistic.

## S3 - Industry SEO Landscape Planning

- [ ] Direct, indirect, and emerging competitors are mapped.
- [ ] Strategic positioning opportunities are explicit.
- [ ] Risks and saturation zones are highlighted.

## S4 - Strategy Reset After Weak SEO Results

- [ ] Existing strategy weaknesses are diagnosed.
- [ ] Competitor-informed alternatives are proposed.
- [ ] Implementation and measurement plan is actionable.
